15 Most Expensive Funko Pops in the World

Publish date: 2024-08-01

Which are the 15 most expensive Funko Pops in the world?

Funko Inc. officially launched cute pop vinyl figures with big heads and tiny bodies called “Funko Pops” exclusively in 2011 in all parts of America.

Since then, the world’s leading toy manufacturer, Funko has produced around 20,000 highly sought-after pop collectibles.

These 3-4 inches tall and extending almost up to 5 inches Funko figures are the custom-built representation of pretty much anything related to pop culture.

The lines of Funko Pops can vary from movies, books, games, shows, comics, animations, celebrities, and broad brands to everything you can ever imagine of.

And, fun fact, Funko Inc. has also permitted you to make yourself as a pop vinyl and put it on a sell. But, first, you would need to visit its headquarters in Everett, Washington.

There are several die-heart fans of these lovely pop vinyl, from kids to adults, who are avid collectors and try to get their hands on each one of them after their release.

With that being all said, let’s take a look at such 15 most expensive Funko Pops in the world that every Funko fanatic covets.

15. Buzz Lightyear Freddy Funko (Metallic)- $3,050

Funko’s mascot, Freddy, with the design of your favorite space ranger from the Toy Story series, made its way at the San Diego Comic-Con exclusively back in 2011.

Besides, Funko produced only twelve pieces of this metallic pop vinyl.

Freddy has a Buzz Lightyear-inspired space suit, purple hair, and a golden crown on his head.

eBay sold this incredibly cool, rare, and highly demanded Funko pop for $3,050 in 2016.

14. Planet Arlia Vegeta- $3,120

Coming to the 14th spot is a Funko Pop that will be the ultimate holy grail for the Dragon Ball Z freaks!

A big round of applause for the Toy Tokyo New York Comic Con, where this exclusive Funko Pop arrived in 2014.

Only limited versions were made available at the New York Comic Con event. Besides, it was a headstrong struggle to add them to your collection.

Compared to previous Vegeta pop vinyl, he looks completely different. He appears bold with his brown hair, cool eye device, smooth skin, and vibrant dress code.

As of this writing, it is worth $3,120.

13. Ghost Rider Freddy Funko (Metallic)- $3,390

Coming to the 13th spot is the coolest Freddy Funko as a ghost rider.

Funko made only twelve pieces of this metallic pop vinyl exclusive at Comic-Con in 2013. And he’s worth $3,390.

They gave this pop vinyl the look of your favorite ghost who rides a motorcycle igniting hellfire.

His face and body are all painted in metallic grey.

Besides, he is peeking at you with his ghostly eyes, laughing with his skeletal teeth, and showing the Ghostrider signature metallic chain on his hand.

12. Frankenberry Freddy Funko (Metallic)- $3,430

This rare pop vinyl features Monster Cereals mascot, Frankenberry, as the Freddy Funko in metallic form.

Funko released only twelve pieces of these Ad Icons at Comic-Con in 2011.

As of 2022, this pop vinyl is worth $3,430.

This Funko pop has Freddy with red hair, a pose of Frankenberry with pink body paint, and pink circles around his eyes.

Per reports, eBay sold two of these pop vinyl for $725 and $3,700 back in 2014 and 2018.

11. Tony Stark Freddy Funko (Metallic)- $3,500

Funko made your favorite Marvel superhero, Iron Man, exclusive as a Freddy Funko at the Comic-Con in 2012.

They released only twelve pieces of this cool pop vinyl.

It’s Freddy in Iron Man’s body. His hair is dark black, he has a mustache, and he is on Tony Stark’s go-to mission mode.

As of 2022, this pop vinyl is one of the most expensive Funko pop in the world, with a price tag of $3,500.

Back in 2016, eBay sold him for $3,499.99. What a bargain!!

10. Boo Berry Funko (GITD)- $3,540

Coming to the tenth spot is the second mascot from Monster Cereals.

Limited to just 24 pieces, Funko released this exclusive and highly craved pop vinyl at Comic-Con in 2011.

Boo Berry Funko is a Pop! Ad Icon.

This glow-in-the-dark pop vinyl features Boo Berry with his sleepy eyes, whole body covered in vivid blue, wearing a pink bow, blue hat, and flaunting his signature monster hand style with a curved smile.

As of this writing, this mini genie-like pop vinyl is worth $3,540.

9. Count Chocula Freddy Funko (Metallic)- $3,990

At this spot, we have another Monster Cereals mascot and Pop! Ad Icon.

Funko released Count Chocula as a Freddy Funko exclusively at Comic-Con in 2011. They limited him up to 480 pieces.

This glossy metallic Freddy pop vinyl is on Count Chocula’s form.

He has a dark skin tone, hair, suit, shoes in a dark orange shade, a black bow, and Chocula teeth, smile, and pose.

Back in 2015, eBay sold him for $725. And now, fast forward to 2022, he is worth $3,990.

8. Boba Fett Freddy Funko (Red Hair)- $3,780

Funko made a couple of Boba Fett Freddy Funkos, but the most expensive one is the one with red hair.

This Freddy Funko is in the form of Star Wars deadly bounty hunter Boba Fett.

He is wearing Boba Fett’s suit with green and silvery paint on it and has a gun in his left hand.

Only 24 pieces of this pop vinyl were made exclusive at Comic-Con in 2014.

As of this writing, this special Bobba Fett Freddy Funko with red hair is worth $3,780.

7. Dumbo (Gold)- $4,180

Disney’s treasure, the flying elephant “Dumbo,” made its debut as a Funko Pop in 2013 at the Comic-Con.

This ultra-rare and exclusive pop vinyl feature Dumbo in a golden version.

Back in 2014, eBay sold it for $710. Later, in 2020, its price skyrocketed up to $6,000.

As of 2022, this golden Dumbo pop vinyl is still one of the most expensive Funko pops, with a price tag of $4,180.

6. Buzz Lightyear Freddy Funko (GITD)- $5,030

On this spot, we have another Buzz Lightyear Freddy Funko, but in your all-time favorite glow-in-the-dark version.

This special and highly sought-after GITD pop vinyl is worth $5,030.

This Freddy Funko pop made its debut at Comic-Con in 2011. Whereas, Funko released only 12 pieces of this pop vinyl.

Freddy has purple hair and is in a Buzz Lightyear spacesuit. Besides, the crown is shining on the top of his head.

5. Frankenberry Freddy Funko (GITD)- $5,610

Coming to the fifth spot is another Frankenberry Freddy Funko, but this time on the super rare glow-in-the-dark version.

This pop vinyl made its exclusive debut at the Comic-Con in 2011. Funko limited him to just 12 pieces.

He’s your cute Frankeberry Freddy Funko, all in pink from head to toe, with white wide eyes and a golden crown to add more style.

As of 2022, this Funko pop is worth $5,610.

4. Dumbo (Clown)- $7,430

If you have watched Disney’s magical adventure, Dumbo, then you better know the scene from which Funko got influenced to make one of its most expensive pop icons.

The look of this pop vinyl comes from the heartbreaking scene of the Dumbo, where the magical elephant is on clown makeup and is forced to perform in a circus.

The clown Dumbo crash-landed at the Comic-Con in 2013. Back in the past, eBay sold this super rare Funko pop for $7,430.

3. Alex Pelurge Clockwork Orange Funko (GITD)- $13,300

Alex is a fictional character from Anthony Burgess’s 1962 crime thriller, A Clockwork Orange.

He is from the dystopian era in England. He is a sadist who is fond of violence and does horrible things to destroy the peace of innocent people like robbing, assaulting, and so on.

Later, the American director Stanley Kubrick transformed the novel into a picture in 1971.

This incredibly rare glow-in-the-dark pop is similar to other versions of him.

He has the same black hat, spooky eyes design, fair skin tone, orange hair, and white polished suit. But one thing’s missing, and that’s the cane.

Yeah, fellas, just without a cane, this pop vinyl has earned the third spot for the most expensive Funko pops in the world. He is worth a staggering $13,300.

2. Jaime Lannister Freddy Funko (Bloody)- $54,997

Holding the second spot is the Game of Thrones badass and witty kingslayer, Jaime Lannister’s Freddy Funko in the bloody version.

Freddy has brown hair. He is wearing a king’s guard armor and holding a sword full of blood in it. His face is also fully covered in blood.

Funko released just 12 pieces of this bloody pop vinyl at Comic-Con in 2013.

Back in 2019, two of them were sold on eBay for $13,500.

As of 2022, the price has escalated up to a whopping $54,997.

1. Stan Lee Signed Pops (Metallic)- $75,000

And finally, we have the world’s most expensive Funko Pop, the ultra-rare and ultra-exclusive Stan Lee signed pops in the metallic form.

Funko paid the tribute to the Marvel Comics writer, the legend who gave life to your treasured superheroes from Spider-Man, X-Man, Iron Man, and Thor to Black Panther after his death in 2018.

Since then, the Funko fans have demanded more Stan Lee pops with his iconic signature on them and have tried to get their hands on at least one of the collections.

But, this 2-pack gold and silver chrome metallic Stan Lee signed Funko pops are extremely valuable than the rest.

As of 2022, these priceless pops cost $75,000 on eBay.

Whereas, once eBay listed it for a staggering $100,000.
