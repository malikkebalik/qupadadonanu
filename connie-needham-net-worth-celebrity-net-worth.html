What is Connie Needham's Net Worth?

Connie Needham is an American actress and dance instructor who has a net worth of $500 thousand. Connie Needham was born in Anaheim, California in December 1959. She is best known for starring as Elizabeth Bradford on the television series Eight Is Enough from 1977 to 1981. She also had a recurring role as Kelly on the TV series Fame from 1982 to 1983.

Needham appeared in episodes of the TV series CHiPs, The Love Boat, Police Squad!, L.A. Law, and Ellen. She starred in the TV movies An Uncommon Love, Eight Is Enough: A Family Reunion, and An Eight Is Enough Wedding. Connie married Eight Is Enough set designer David Needham in 1979 but the couple divorced in 2005 after having two daughters.
