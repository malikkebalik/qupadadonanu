NBA

After a standout freshman year at Memphis, Rose was selected as Chicago Bulls’ first overall pick in the 2008 draft.

Since Michael Jordan, Rose was the first Chicago Bulls draftee to score 10 points or more in his first ten games.

Rose was repeatedly awarded the Rookie of The Month until he rose to Rookie of the Year.

Similarly, in the 2009-10 season, he would be elected to his first NBA All-Star game.

Likewise, in the 2010-11 season, Rose would be named the NBA MVP, joining Michael Jordan as the only Bulls player to do so.

In the process, Derrick also became the youngest NBA MVP at just 23 years of age.
