Emily Rios Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-07-24

Emily Rios:

$1 million

Emily Rios is an American actress. Her Hollywood career started in the mid-2000s. She made her film debut in Quinceañera. She has since had roles in the films The Blue Hour, Vicious Circle, Down For Life, and The Winning Season. On television, her first notable role was in Men of a Certain Age as Maria. She next appeared as Epyck Sanders in Friday Night Lights. In the 2010s, she became further known for playing Andrea Cantillo in Breaking Bad. At the same time, she played Angela Reilly in Private Practice. Afterwards, she joined the main cast of The Bridge as Adriana Mendez. More recently, she had recurring roles as Betty Chessani in True Detective and as Ximena Vasconcelos in From Dusk till Dawn. This year, she stars on the TV series Snowfall as Lucia.
