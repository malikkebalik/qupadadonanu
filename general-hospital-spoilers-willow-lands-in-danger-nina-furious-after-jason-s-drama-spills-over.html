General Hospital Spoilers: Willow Lands in Danger Nina Furious After Jasons Drama Spills Over

Publish date: 2024-09-04

General Hospital (GH) spoilers reveal that Willow Corinthos (Katelyn MacMullen) could be in trouble now that she’s signed up to help a fugitive who’s caught up in something that’s obviously quite dangerous.

Jason Morgan (Steve Burton) didn’t ask for Willow to become part of all this, but she volunteered anyway.

As a result, Willow offered Jason some much-needed medical attention, but she’s also way too close to all the peril.

At the end of the March 13 episode, Willow returned to the boathouse and found Jason sleeping with his weapon at the ready.

It felt almost ominous as Willow covered Jason up and welcomed him home.

Could GH writers be hinting about the danger spilling over onto Willow?

In addition to helping a wanted man, Willow is connecting herself to all the mob drama that Jason’s tangled up in.

What happens if Willow gets hurt because she’s too close to this Jason situation? Willow’s life could be in jeopardy, so Nina Corinthos (Cynthia Watros) may end up furious if she gets updates on that news.

It’s easy to imagine Nina lashing out at Michael Corinthos (Chad Duell) for not doing his part to protect Willow.

Jason could also get an earful down the road along with Carly Spencer (Laura Wright) since she’s Jason’s number one fan.

If Nina starts slamming Jason, that could be a quick path to infuriating Carly for the umpteenth time.

It’d be a way for TBTB to keep Carly and Nina’s feud going, so we’ll see how it all plays out.

Whatever the case, Nina may have some good reasons to fear for Willow depending on where this story goes next.
