What is Glenn Jones' Net Worth?

Glenn Jones is an American R&B and soul singer who has a net worth of $700 thousand. Glenn Jones was born in Jacksonville, Florida in 1962. He started out as a gospel singer with the group The Modulations and was featured on their albums With a Made Up Mind in 1978 and Feel the Fire in 1980. Glenn Jones released his debut studio album Everybody Loves a Winner in 1983.

His album Finesse was released in 1984 and reached #16 on the US R&B chart. Jones released the album Take It from Me in 1986 and his album Glenn Jones was released in 1987 and reached #16 on the US R&B chart. He also released the albums All For You in 1990, Here I Go Again in 1992, Here I Am in 1994, It's Time in 1998, Feels Good in 2002, and Forever: Timeless R&B Classics in 2006. Jones had a #1 hit on the US R&B chart with his single "Here I Go Again".
