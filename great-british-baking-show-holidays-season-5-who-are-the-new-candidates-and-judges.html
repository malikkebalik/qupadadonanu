What Makes this Holiday Episode Distinct from the Rest of the Season?

Instead of a complete season, the Christmas special only has two episodes. Each two-hour episode features a baking competition with a seasonal twist. Two episodes in Season 5 will celebrate the holidays: one on Christmas and one on New Year’s Eve, the same as in previous seasons.

Traditional European Christmas desserts such as fruit crumbles, Christmas puddings, and miniature panettones were created by the participants in the 2016 episode.

Paul Hollywood, notorious for his harsh criticism, reportedly attempts to tone it down for the holiday episode. Will I be as strict while making decisions? In a word, no. Isn’t it the holiday season? That’s what the Hollywood teaser says.

The Great British Baking Show: Holidays Season 5: Who are the Candidates?
