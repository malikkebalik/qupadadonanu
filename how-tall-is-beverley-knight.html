Publish date: 2024-09-03

Beverley Knight's Height

5ft 3 ½ (161.3 cm)

British Singer. She can look taller in videos (namely keep this fire burning) because she's in heels and the back dancers are in sneakers. In Ok Magazine (June 2010) she is quoted saying, "I'm quite short, just shy of 5ft 4in - and I can run up and down a stage in heels, no problem." and on twitter she said: " I am 5ft 3." and "Sending the biggest hug a 5ft 3 1/2 inch woman can muster". She also said her sister was 5ft 10.
