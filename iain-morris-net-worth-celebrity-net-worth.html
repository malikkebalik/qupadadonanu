Iain Morris net worth and salary: Iain Morris is an English writer who has a net worth of $10 million. Iain Morris was born in Woking, Surrey, England in August 1973. He is best known for creating The Inbetweeners along with writing partner Damon Beesley. Morris co-hosts a radio show in London with stand-up comedian Jimmy Carr.

Iain Morris has served as an executive producer for the TV series Free Agents in 2009, The Persuasionists in 2010, The Inbetweeners from 2008 to 2010 and in 2012, Drifters from 2013 to 2016, White Gold in 2017, and Ill Behaviour in 2017. He has also written and or produced the movies The Inbetweeners Movie and The Inbetweeners 2 and Jimmy Carr: Stand Up, Jimmy Carr: Comedian, and Jimmy Carr: In Concert. Morris directed the movies The Inbetweeners 2 and The Festival. He won a British Comedy Award and a National Television Award for The Inbetweeners and has been nominated for multiple BAFTA Awards.
