Who Is LaMelo Ball?

Born in Charlotte, North Carolina on August 22, 2001, LaMelo LaFrance Ball is a professional basketball player in the NBA for the Charlotte Hornets (NBA). In the 2020 NBA draught, the Hornets used the third overall pick on him. In 2021, Ball was crowned NBA Rookie of the Year and in 2022, he was selected as an NBA All-Star.

LaMelo Ball Early Life

Ball’s parents, both of whom played basketball at the collegiate level, gave birth to him in Anaheim, California. LaVar Ball began coaching his son, Ball, in basketball the moment he could run. When he was four years old, he began playing basketball alongside his elder brothers Lonzo and LiAngelo, against competitors who were decades his senior.

At the age of five, he played flag football with his brothers, but he mostly stuck to basketball.
