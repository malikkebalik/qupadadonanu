What is Jazzy B's Net Worth?

Jazzy B is a Punjabi language Bhangra singer and songwriter who has a net worth of $50 million. Jazzy B was born in Nawanshahr, Punjab, India in April 1975. His family moved to Canada when he was five years old and he now lives in England. Jazzy B released his debut studio album Ghugian Da Jorra in 1993.

He has also released the albums The Canadian Spice in 1994, Folk 'N Funky in 1995, Folkal Attraction in 1997, All Eyez on Me in 1999, Stayin' Real Surma in 2000, Oh Kehri in 2001, Terra Roop in 2002, Romeo in 2004, Rambo in 2008, and Maharajas in 2011. Jazzy B is referred to as the "Crown Prince of Bhangra" and has also collaborated with many other artists. He has won several awards and also appeared in television and films.
