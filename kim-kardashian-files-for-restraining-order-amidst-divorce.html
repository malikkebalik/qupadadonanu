Kim Kardashian Files For Restraining Order Amidst Divorce

Publish date: 2024-08-30

For months, rumors have been swirling that Kim Kardashian and Kanye West were ready for divorce, and earlier this month it was all but confirmed as the truth.

By now, everyone knows that the two are officially over after a six year marriage. But amidst all of the drama, Kim has filed for a restraining order.
