What is Peter Murphy's Net Worth?

Peter Murphy is an English singer and musician who has a net worth of $10 million. Peter Murphy was born in Northampton, Northamptonshire, England in July 1957. He was the vocalist of the rock band Bauhaus before becoming a solo artist. He has been nicknamed the "Godfather of Goth" and plays guitar, keyboards, percussion, and melodica. Bauhaus formed in 1978 and released their debut studio album In the Flat Field in 1980.

The band released the album Mask in 1981 and their album The Sky's Gone Out was released in 1982 and reached #4 in the UK. They also released the albums Burning from the Inside in 1983 and Go Away White in 2008. Murphy has released the solo albums Should the World Fail to Fall Apart, Love Hysteria, Deep, Holy Smoke, Cascade, Dust, Unshattered, Ninth, and Lion. His single "Cuts You Up" reached #1 on the US Modern Rock chart.
