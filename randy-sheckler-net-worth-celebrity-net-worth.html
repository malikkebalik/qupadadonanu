What is Randy Sheckler's Net Worth?

Randy Sheckler is an American entrepreneur who has a net worth of $4 million. Randy Sheckler is probably most famous for being the father of professional skateboarder Ryan Sheckler. A child prodigy, Ryan Sheckler honed his amazing skating skills in the park and on the street before eventually turning pro in 2003. On the way, he has won various titles including Gravity Games, Vans Triple Crown, Slam City Jam, and World Cup of Skateboarding, among others. Even though only a teenager, he became a world-renowned athlete especially after appearing on the reality show "The Life of Ryan", which premiered on MTV in 2007.

Since, he has landed parts and appearances on both the small and the big screen. His international fame only facilitated the process of inking lucrative sponsorship deals with sound brands, like Volcom Clothing, Red Bull, Oakley Eyewear, Panasonic, Independent, Etnies, Nixon, Grizzly, Ethika, Go Pro, Andale and Plan B. In addition, he started his own clothing line called 'RS'. Aside from generating an abundant fortune, Randy Sheckler set up 'Sheckler Foundation', a charity organization that mainly helps injured athletes and children in need. Not surprising at all, Sheckler earned a place in Fox Weekly's '15 Most influential skateboarders of all-time'.
