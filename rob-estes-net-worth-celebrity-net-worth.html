What is Rob Estes' Net Worth?

Rob Estes is an American actor who has a net worth of $3 million. Rob Estes was born in Norfolk, Virginia in July 1963. Estes originally intended to be a stunt double before he got into acting. From 1986 to 1987 he starred as Glenn Gallagher on the soap opera Days of Our Lives. From 1991 to 1995 Estes starred as Sgt. Chris Lorenzo on the television series Silk Stalkings. He starred as Kyle McBride on the TV series Melrose Place from 1993 to 1999.

From 1999 to 2000 Estes starred as Oliver Browne on the series Suddenly Susan and he starred as John Hemming on the series Providence in 2000. He starred as Sean Cole on the TV series The Evidence in 2006. Estes had a recurring role as Nick Townsend on CSI: Miami from 2006 to 2007 and starred as Tom Hogan on the television series Women's Murder Club from 2007 to 2008. From 2008 to 2010 he starred as Harry Wilson on the TV series 90210. He had a recurring role as Rob Maroney on the series Necessary Roughness in 2012. In 2009 and 2010 he was nominated for Teen Choice Awards for 90210. Rob was married to Melrose Place actress Josie Bissett from 1992 to 2006.
