'The Umbrella Academy' Cast Net Worth and Who Makes the Most From the Show

Publish date: 2024-08-30

The Umbrella Academy Season 3 premieres on Netflix in 2022. While likely to introduce more characters, the story will follow the Hargreeves as they learn more about their “siblings.”

The actors behind the protagonists have undoubtedly garnered wealth since the series began, including those who weren’t famous when it started. Here’s a look at the net worth and salary of The Umbrella Academy cast.

The salaries of the ‘The Umbrella Academy’ cast

While their starting salary is unknown, the cast of The Umbrella Academy makes more now than in 2019. Variety reports that the stars negotiated a pay raise for the upcoming season. Now, they will make $200,000 per episode. 

Like the previous seasons, the third one consists of 10 episodes. In total, each core cast member will have made roughly $2 million after the finale episode. However, one of the lead stars has a higher salary than their co-stars. 

Elliot Page was not a part of the group’s effort to get a pay raise. The Juno star already got a larger paycheck due to his fame before the series. Nevertheless, Page supported his cast members’ decision to renegotiate salaries. 

The amount the stars make could change if the show gets a fourth season. Thanks to its popularity, the actors have a high net worth. 

Which ‘The Umbrella Academy’ star has the highest net worth?

Naturally, Page has the highest net worth among the primary The Umbrella Academy cast members. According to Celebrity Net Worth, their wealth is around $8 Million. Previously, the publication reported the Inception actor’s net worth closer to $14 million, but that has since been adjusted.

Meanwhile, Tom Hopper’s Celebrity Net Worth page shows that he has a net worth of $5 million. The actor has been in other popular shows before taking on the role of Luther in The Umbrella Academy. He had minor roles in Merlin and Game of Thrones, earning money from multiple films last year. 

Fans know David Castañeda best as the rebellious troublemaker Diego. Before the show, he landed a recurring role in the show Switched at Birth. The star also appeared in a few episodes of Jane the Virgin. Castañeda’s estimated net worth is unclear since various sources report different estimates. 

Co-star Emmy Raver-Lampman thrived on Broadway before she joined the Netflix series. She had roles in productions like Wicked. Multiple sources report various numbers, so her exact wealth is not clear. 

The net worth of the rest of the main cast

Robert Sheehan has made quite a bit of money throughout his career. Celebrity Net Worth estimates the actor’s worth to be around $2 million. Plenty of his wealth comes from acting, but he also is an author of a book called Disappearing Acts. 

Justin H. Min has been in a few shows, but his most prominent role is Ben Hargreeves. The star’s net worth is not conclusive since different sources list various numbers. He likely makes a considerable amount of money like his fellow cast members. 

Fans enjoy watching Aidan Gallagher bring Five to life. He has had a short acting career so far, but he likely may continue to find success. According to his Celebrity Net Worth page, he appeared on a Nickelodeon show from 2014 to 2018. 

Gallagher has received a couple of award nominations and accrued significant wealth. Currently, he has a net worth of $2 million. With a series as popular as The Umbrella Academy, their net worth could increase as time goes on.
