What is Vic Fuentes' Net Worth?

Vic Fuentes is an American singer and musician who has a net worth of $1 million. Vic Fuentes was born in San Diego, California in February 1983. He is best known for being the lead singer and rhythm guitarist of the band Pierce the Veil. Fuentes played with the band Before Today on their album A Celebration of an Ending in 2004. He formed the post-hardcore band Pierce the Veil with his brother Mike Fuentes in 2006 in San Diego.

Their debut studio album A Flair for the Dramatic was released in 2007. The band's second studio album Selfish Machines was released in 2010 and reached #1 on the US Heatseekers chart. Pierce the Veil's third studio album Collide with the Sky was released in 2012 and reached #1 on the US Heatseekers, US Alternative, and US Rock charts. The band's most successful singles are "King for a Day" and "The Divine Zero". Fuentes also appeared on the Isles & Glaciers 2010 album The Hearts of Lonely People.
