Where to find dog-friendly restaurants in Roswell

Publish date: 2024-09-30

Want to take man's best friend out to happy hour? You can at these Roswell restaurants with pet-friendly patios.

Last November, the Georgia Department of Public Health lightened up their rules regarding dogs. In all counties except for Fulton County, dogs are allowed within outdoor dining areas as long as those are accessible from the outdoors. Read more about the new rules here.

Here are seven restaurants in Roswell that welcome dogs of all shapes and sizes:

Lucky’s Burger & Brew

Lucky is a real dog, a Golden Retriever who lives with the owners. He's the logo too. The menu is true to the restaurant's name, but there’s more than burgers and beer to choose from.

1144 Alpharetta St. Roswell, GA 30075; (770) 518-5695; luckys.marshallwebdesignportfolio.com

Peach & the Porkchop

The family-friendly destination in Sweet Apple Village is both a sports pub and fine-dining restaurant. Read our review here.

12040 Etris Rd, Roswell, GA 30075; (770) 696-5409; www.peachandtheporkchop.com

Plum Cafe

This restaurant is perfect for those who love organic, vegan and gluten-free meals and fluffy dogs. Read our review here.

1055 Canton St #110, Roswell, GA 30075; (770) 518-7586; www.plumcafe.net/home

Roux On Canton

Get hushpuppies with your own pooch at this Cajun-Creole inspired restaurant on Canton Street.

946 Canton St. Roswell, GA 30075; (770) 993-0007; rouxoncanton.com

Salt Factory Pub

This English-inspired gastropub welcomes dogs on their patio.

952 Canton St, Roswell, GA 30075; (770) 998-4850; http://saltfactoryroswell.fhfoodtradinggroup.com/

1920 Tavern

Despite the swankiness and speakeasy vibe, all visitors will enjoy the playfulness of this spot.

948 Canton St, Roswell, GA 30075; (770) 998-3114; 1920tavern.com

Vin 25 Bistro & Wine Bar

This full service, chef-driven restaurant with a serious wine program also lets dogs hang out with their owners on the patio. Read our review here.

25 Plum Tree St, Roswell, GA 30075; (770) 628-0411; vin25.com
