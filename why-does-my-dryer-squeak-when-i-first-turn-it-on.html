Why does my dryer squeak when I first turn it on?

Publish date: 2024-07-29

Depending on the make and model of your dryer, squeaks may be caused by a worn tub support roller, worn rear drum mount bearings, worn belt idler pulley, worn front glides or felt, worn blower squirrel cage bearings or even worn drive motor bearings.
