He commenced his career in comedy by writing and producing a comic book called 101 Things to Definitely Not Do If You Want to Get a Chick. This book was a funny dating advice book for men. After that, he worked for an American late-night talk show, Late Show.

Then he moved to The Groundlings and worked with famous actors like Jim Rash, Maya Rudolph, and Cheryl Hines, and also started writing like Action and The Army Show. Apart from all this, he also tried to do stand-up comedy many times at events like open mic nights.

After that, he Writes for an American sitcom called 3rd Rock from the Sun (1996), the Television Show, The Jenny McCarthy Show (1997), and the American sitcom That 70’s Show (1998). Later, he received the offer to be on the comedy show Saturday Night Live and he joined the cast of Saturday Night Live for the twenty-eight season.

In 2007, he also wrote and co-starred in the film The Brothers Solomon. Following this, he featured in many TV Series like The WWE Raw (2010), Cleveland Show (2009-13), American Dad (2009-15), and Conan (2010-13).

In 2012, he played supporting roles in three films Rock of Ages (2012), That’s My Boy (2012), and The Watch (2012). Will was cast in television series like 7 Days in Hell (2015) and Maya & Marty (2016), action-comedy film Keanu (2016).

He also lent his voice in many animated movies like Aqua Unit Patrol Squad 1 (2011), Cloudy with a Chance of Meatballs (2009), and Cloudy with a Chance of Meatballs 2 (2013), My Life as a Courgette (2017), and The Lego Movie 2: The Second Part (2019).

He is not married yet but got engaged to Olivia Modling in 2020. Before this, he also dated actress Sarah Chalke.
